Meet Tammy | Owner & Founder

Tammy has worked in the dental field all over the US and Canada for more than 30 years. In that time, she has worked as a Dental Assistant, Hygiene Assistant, Front Desk/Patient Coordinator, Treatment Coordinator and Office Manager. This vast experience enables her to understand every department in a dental office. She has a passion for dentistry and knows how to get your team excited about going digital. She has extensive knowledge of the Eaglesoft and Revenue Well software programs. She loves updating dental offices!
